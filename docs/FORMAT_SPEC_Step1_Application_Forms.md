# Step 1: Application Forms - Output Format Specification

**Version**: 2.0
**Purpose**: Exact format specification for Step 1 outputs (360 SIS and CNEN applications)

---

## Overview

Step 1 generates program-specific application documents from the Executive Brief (Step 0). Two standard formats are supported:

1. **360 SIS Application** - Social impact startups and accelerators
2. **CNEN Application** - Research institutions and technology transfer

---

## Required Output Structure

### Document Metadata (Required for Both Formats)

```markdown
**Program Name:** [Name of accelerator/program]
**Application Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Application ID:** [If assigned, otherwise "Pending"]
**Applicant Type:** [Organization type]
```

---

## 360 SIS Application Format

### Document Structure (6 Sections)

| Section | Title | Content | Page Estimate |
|---------|-------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Project Overview | Name, summary, problem, solution | 1-2 pages |
| 2 | Impact & Theory of Change | Beneficiaries, ToC, metrics, SDGs | 1-2 pages |
| 3 | Business Model | Revenue, costs, sustainability | 1 page |
| 4 | Team & Capacity | Founders, gaps, advisors | 1 page |
| 5 | Traction & Validation | Stage, milestones, evidence | 1 page |
| 6 | Funding & Resources | History, ask, runway | 1 page |

### Character/Sentence Limits (Strictly Enforced)

| Field | Limit | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Tagline | 15 words max | One compelling sentence |
| Project Summary | 2-3 sentences, 500 chars | Problem + solution + beneficiaries |
| Problem Statement | 3-5 sentences, 750 chars | Solution-neutral, quantified |
| Proposed Solution | 3-5 sentences, 750 chars | Approach + differentiation |
| Theory of Change | 5-7 sentences, 1000 chars | IF-THEN-WHICH-RESULTING logic |
| Revenue Model | 3-5 sentences, 600 chars | How money is made |
| Sustainability Path | 3-4 sentences, 500 chars | Path to break-even |
| Validation Evidence | 3-5 sentences, 750 chars | Specific proof points |

### Quality Checklist for 360 SIS

- [ ] All 6 sections complete
- [ ] All character limits respected
- [ ] Problem statement is solution-neutral
- [ ] Impact metrics are SMART
- [ ] Theory of Change has IF-THEN logic
- [ ] SDG alignment documented
- [ ] Revenue streams quantified
- [ ] Team backgrounds are specific
- [ ] Milestones have dates and evidence
- [ ] Funding ask is justified

## CNEN Application Format

### Document Structure (6 Sections)

| Section | Title | Content | Page Estimate |
|---------|-------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Project Description | Title, summary, problem, innovation | 2 pages |
| 2 | Team & Expertise | PI, team, collaborations | 1-2 pages |
| 3 | Market & Commercialization | Market, competition, IP | 1-2 pages |
| 4 | Technology & Methodology | TRL, methodology, risks | 1-2 pages |
| 5 | Resources & Budget | Budget, resources, justification | 1-2 pages |
| 6 | Timeline & Deliverables | Timeline, milestones, outcomes | 1 page |

### Character/Sentence Limits (Strictly Enforced)

| Field | Limit | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Project Title | 100 chars max | Official title |
| Executive Summary | 5-7 sentences, 1500 chars | All 5 required elements |
| Problem Statement | 4-6 sentences, 1000 chars | Gap in knowledge/capability |
| Innovation Description | 4-6 sentences, 1000 chars | Differentiation from state-of-art |
| Target Market | 3-5 sentences, 750 chars | TAM/SAM with growth |
| Commercialization Pathway | 4-6 sentences, 1000 chars | Research to market path |
| Methodology | 5-7 sentences, 1500 chars | Research approach |
| Funding Justification | 3-5 sentences, 750 chars | Why funding is needed |

### Quality Checklist for CNEN

- [ ] All 6 sections complete
- [ ] All character limits respected
- [ ] TRL assessment is accurate
- [ ] Innovation is differentiated
- [ ] Methodology is rigorous
- [ ] Budget is itemized and justified
- [ ] Timeline has measurable milestones
- [ ] Risks identified with mitigations
- [ ] IP strategy documented
- [ ] Team expertise matches project needs

## Output Specifications

### File Format
- Primary: Microsoft Word (.docx)
- Secondary: Markdown (.md) for version control

### File Naming Convention

**360 SIS:**
```
[CompanyName]_360SIS_Application_YYYYMMDD.docx
```

**CNEN:**
```
[ProjectName]_CNEN_Application_YYYYMMDD.docx
```

### Page Estimates
- 360 SIS: 8-12 pages
- CNEN: 10-15 pages

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Exceeding character limits → ✅ Count characters carefully
2. ❌ Solution in problem statement → ✅ Problem should be solution-neutral
3. ❌ Vague impact metrics → ✅ Use SMART metrics
4. ❌ Missing evidence citations → ✅ Reference specific documents
5. ❌ Generic team descriptions → ✅ Specific backgrounds and experience
6. ❌ Incomplete budget breakdown → ✅ Itemize all categories
7. ❌ Unrealistic timelines → ✅ Based on evidence and constraints
8. ❌ Missing risk mitigations → ✅ Every risk needs a strategy
