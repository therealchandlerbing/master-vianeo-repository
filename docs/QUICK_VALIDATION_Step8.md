## COMMON FIXES (2-minute corrections)

**All Favorable ratings?**
- Review competitors ‚Üí they're likely Unfavorable or Neutral
- Check regulators ‚Üí often Neutral until engaged
- Add "[validate]" for unconfirmed assumptions

**Generic categories only?**
- ‚ùå "Distribution partners"
- ‚úÖ "Amazon Marketplace, Shopify App Store, Direct Sales Team"
- Research specific names in the industry

**Players vs Influencers confused?**
| Type | Relationship | Examples |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Player** | Transacts with you | Suppliers, channels, customers |
| **Influencer** | Shapes decisions but doesn't transact | Regulators, analysts, associations |

**Missing competitors?**
- Include direct competitors (same solution)
- Include indirect competitors (different solution, same need)
- Include "Do Nothing" as a competitor

## ACCEPTABILITY RATING DEFINITIONS

| Rating | Definition | Color |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Favorable** | Likely to support, aligned interests | Green (#10b981) |
| **Neutral** | No strong position, persuadable | Yellow (#f59e0b) |
| **Unfavorable** | Likely to resist, conflicting interests | Red (#ef4444) |

**Evidence requirements:**
- Favorable: Direct contact confirming interest, or clear strategic alignment
- Neutral: No engagement yet, or mixed signals
- Unfavorable: Direct rejection, competitive conflict, or regulatory barrier
