# Step 8: Players & Influencers

## Overview
Ecosystem acceptability analysis identifying key players and influencers in the innovation ecosystem.

## Purpose
Map and analyze stakeholders who can enable or impede project success to develop engagement strategies.

## Player Categories

### Direct Players
- Customers and users
- Partners and suppliers
- Competitors
- Distributors and channels

### Influencers
- Industry thought leaders
- Regulatory bodies
- Standards organizations
- Media and analysts
- Investor community

### Gatekeepers
- Technology platforms
- Distribution networks
- Certification authorities
- Professional associations

## Analysis Dimensions

### Influence Mapping
- Power level
- Interest level
- Position (supporter/blocker/neutral)
- Influence mechanisms

### Acceptability Assessment
- Stakeholder readiness
- Adoption barriers
- Engagement requirements
- Risk factors

### Engagement Strategy
- Priority stakeholders
- Communication approaches
- Value propositions
- Risk mitigation

## Output
- Stakeholder map
- Influence matrix
- Acceptability scorecard
- Engagement strategy
