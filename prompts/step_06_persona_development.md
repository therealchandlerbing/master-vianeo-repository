# Step 6: Persona Development

## Overview
Create evidence-based user personas with validation badges to represent key user segments.

## Purpose
Develop detailed, validated personas that guide product development, marketing, and customer engagement strategies.

## Persona Components

### Demographic Information
- Age, gender, location
- Education and background
- Professional role
- Economic status

### Psychographic Profile
- Goals and motivations
- Pain points and frustrations
- Values and beliefs
- Behaviors and habits

### Contextual Factors
- Usage scenarios
- Environmental constraints
- Social influences
- Decision-making process

### Evidence-Based Validation
- Data sources
- Research methods
- Validation level
- Confidence score

## Validation Badges

Personas receive badges indicating:
- **Evidence Level**: Amount of research supporting the persona
- **Validation Status**: Degree of market validation
- **Representativeness**: How well the persona represents the segment
- **Stability**: Likelihood the persona remains relevant over time

## Output
- Detailed persona documents
- Validation scorecards
- Usage guidelines
- Design implications
